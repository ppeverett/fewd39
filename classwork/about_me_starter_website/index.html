About Peter

Peter Everett grew up near Boston and is a native New Englander. For much of his career, Peter has been a nomadic digital worker, traveling primarily on the West coast contributing to many digital products as a product manager, UX leader and marketer.

Peter loves being at the inception point of an idea and he has a passion for working with consumers and businesses who generate and leverage data.

First thing about me is that I love to code and develop great software. I get so excited by the thought of using code to create something novel!
I also love to chill out and enjoy my downtime. In my spare time I motorcycle, ride bikes, hike, and travel whenever I get the chance.
At heart I am a teacher. I love to be in the classroom and I really enjoy helping my students solve problems and meet their goals. It's awesome!
